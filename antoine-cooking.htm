Three Belgian Recipes

 

Chocolate Mousse

 

Ingredients for eight:

200g (~7oz) of dark chocolate (Côte d’Or recommended)

6 eggs

100g (~3.5oz) of butter

100g (~3.5oz) of powdered sugar

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Instructions:

Brake chocolate into small pieces and add butter. Microwave to melt.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Separate the egg whites from the yolks.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

In a large bowl put the sugar and the egg yolks mix together.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Add slowly the melted chocolate to the yolk and sugar mix. Whisk the egg whites with a pinch of salt.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Gently fold the egg white with a spatula one scoop at a time.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Whip the cream until firm.

 


 

Gently mix it with everything else. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours.

 


 

 

Liège Waffles

 

Ingredients for 20 waffles:

800g  (~1.75 lbs) flour

1 pack of instant dry yeast

3 packs of vanilla sugar or 4 ½ Tsp vanilla extract

¼ L (~8.5oz) milk

230g (~8oz) butter, softened

230g (~8oz) Pearl sugar or raw cane sugar

115g whole cane sugar

1tsp cinnamon

Pinch of salt

4 eggs (or 5 if using whole flour)

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Instructions:

Activate yeast in 2tbs of warm water.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Mix flour, activated yeast, eggs, milk, vanilla sugar, and cinnamon in a bowl.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Cover and let it rise for half an hour (volume should double). Add butter, pinch of salt, and sugar.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Bake in a preheated waffle iron (1 scoop per waffle) until golden brown.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Apple Cake

Ingredients for eight:

200g (~7oz) butter

225g (~8oz) sugar (powdered sugar: light brown sugar, 50:50)

3 eggs

225g (~8oz) white whole flour

2 packs of vanilla sugar or 3 Tsp vanilla extract

8g (~1/2 Tbsp) backing powder

3 apples (Golden Delicious or Gala)

Pinch of salt

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Instructions:

In a bowl, work on the butter to soften it. Add sugar, vanilla sugar, and mix.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Add salt and eggs one by one, and mix.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Mix in the flour and baking powder.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Preheat oven to 390 F (or 365 F convection oven). Peel and slice the apples. Put a layer of apple slices at the bottom of greased 9-inch round cake pan.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Pour 1/2 of batter on top of the apple add a second layer of apple slices.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Pour the second half of batter and cover with apple slices.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

Bake until wooden pick inserted in center comes out clean, about 45min. Let it cool and turn it over on a serving plate. Sprinkle with powdered sugar to decorate.
